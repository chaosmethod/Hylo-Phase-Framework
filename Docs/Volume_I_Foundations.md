# Part I. Executive Canonical Statement

## 1\. What HPF is

The **Hylo Phase Framework (HPF)**, previously presented as the **Holographic Projection Framework (HPF)**, is a **regulatory execution framework for bounded physical evolution on a finite-capacity substrate**.

Its job is to:

1. enforce hard execution constraints,
2. track bounded substrate load,
3. distinguish legality from validity,
4. govern expert routing and lawful handoff,
5. determine when effective theories remain trustworthy,
6. route execution to deeper substrate-side authority when an effective regime loses validity or legality.

In operating language: **HPF is the physics OS.**

## 2\. What MDEA is

**MDEA** is the routing kernel inside HPF. It evaluates admissible experts, scores them, and assigns local descriptive authority without altering the higher-level legality rules imposed by HPF.

## 3\. What geometry / gravity is

Geometry / gravity is a **downstream effective expert regime**. It is:

* downstream of HPF,
* downstream of MDEA,
* subordinate to legality constraints,
* valid only in a bounded coarse-grained regime,
* required to hand off when validity margin is lost.

Geometry is therefore not the regulator. It is a routed expert that remains useful only where HPF still permits it.

## 4\. What QPRCA is

**QPRCA** is the deeper substrate-side expert / handoff destination used when effective geometry is no longer sufficient or when hard legality conditions require substrate-side execution.

## 5\. Current one-sentence package status

HPF/MDEA is now a layered, truth-status-controlled regulatory execution framework with a locked legality/validity split, a locked two-wall architecture, a live routed-expert bridge stack, a candidate-locked 4-bit substrate grammar, a corrected no-`/2` radial law, a derived `S\\\_cap` closure spine, a candidate-locked shell selector `n=220`, and a separated Lambda branch and dark-matter branch, while strongest-form microscopic legality closure, assumption-free governor uniqueness, lower-wall constitutive uniqueness, and strongest-form Lambda closure without external cosmological anchoring remain open.
